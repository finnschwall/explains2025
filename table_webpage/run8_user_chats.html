There were 768 combinations, 138 chats and 105984 prompts evaluated in this run.

False positive and false negative rates have been removed as a global interpretation is complicated. Due to the study design most users only looked at a subsection of the data points. The chats per data point are also not homogeneously distributed e.g. usually a user asks more questions on the first encountered data points. As data points and their correct classification do not follow a specific pattern, the FN and FP rates would need to be compared against the users progress and chat distributions.
